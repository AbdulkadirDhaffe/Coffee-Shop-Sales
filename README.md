# Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Coffee Shop Sales data using Microsoft Excel to generate meaningful business insights and build an interactive dashboard for decision-making.

The goal of this project was to clean raw sales data, transform it into a structured format, perform analysis using Pivot Tables and formulas, and finally create a professional dashboard that provides clear insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.

This project simulates a real business scenario where management needs quick and accurate reports to improve operations, increase revenue, and understand customer preferences.

---

## Objective

The main objective of this project is to analyze coffee shop sales performance and answer important business questions such as:

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which store locations have the highest customer footfall?
* What are the busiest hours of the day?
* Which weekdays generate the most sales?
* What product sizes do customers prefer most?
* Which product categories contribute the most to total revenue?

The final dashboard helps decision-makers quickly understand business performance and identify growth opportunities.

## Data Cleaning Process

Several cleaning and transformation steps were performed using Power Query:

### 1. Time Formatting

The original transaction time included unnecessary date values. The time column was cleaned to keep only the actual time.

### 2. Product Detail Cleaning

Size abbreviations such as LG (Large), RG (Regular), and SM (Small) were removed from product details.

### 3. Remove Extra Spaces

The TRIM function was used to remove leading and trailing spaces from product names.

### 4. Created New Columns

Additional columns were created for better analysis:

* Size
* Total Bill
* Month Name
* Day Name
* Hour
* Month Number
* Day of Week Number

These columns improved filtering, sorting, and dashboard performance.

## KPI Measures

Four main KPIs were created to summarize business performance:

### Total Sales

Total revenue generated from all transactions.

### Total Footfall

Total number of customer transactions.

### Average Bill per Person

Average amount spent per customer.

### Average Order per Person

Average quantity of items ordered per customer.

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business health.

## Key Business Insights

Some major findings from the analysis include:

* Morning hours showed the highest customer activity
* Coffee products generated the highest revenue
* Certain store locations had significantly higher footfall
* Large and Regular sizes were the most preferred by customers
* Weekday sales outperformed weekend sales in some locations
* A small number of products generated a large portion of total sales

These insights help management make better operational and strategic decisions.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

* Increase inventory for top-selling products
* Improve staffing during peak business hours
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing locations
* Promote low-performing products through offers
* Use customer buying patterns to improve pricing strategy
* Optimize product availability based on size demand

These actions can improve both profitability and customer satisfaction.
